How to Play:
1.This is a guess the logo game with 20 logos to identify.
2.Each logo displayed will have four options.
  Out of them only one will be correct.
3.If you select the right option your score will be incremented
  and next logo will be displayed.
4.If you select the wrong option you would lose. 
  You have only one life. 
             So play well!!!!
